(In)frequently Asked Questions

Where should I move after I stop living in the dorms?

Somewhere with a washer and dryer in the building or you will want to do very bad things.

How do I manage my time?

Sleep when you are tired and the work will get done at some point. Spend time creating relationships with people because those are worth more at the end of the day. And when you fail your physics exam because you went to see King Princess on a Wednesday night, remember your GPA isn’t forever. Lesbianism is forever.

How do I deal with roommates?

Be honest about your feelings and leave your bad vibes at the door. We can taste your negativity, Brittany. It’s bitter and salty.

How do you handle a long distance relationship in college?

Break up with him, girl! He yelled at you when you got your nose pierced! You have too many people to meet to be sitting on FaceTime in the lounge while everyone else is chugging Four Lokos upstairs.

How does NYU Classes work?

You click a class from the top panel and wait for it to load and then you die

How do I have good hygiene if I hate doing laundry?

No matter how good your hygiene is, you will inevitably get lice from a girl who tried on a cow head at a halloween store.

I’m nervous to hang out with my school friends outside of class? What should we do for fun?

Hang out with them even if you’re scared because they’re cool or pretty! It might go well

How do I tell my best friend I don't want to live with them next year?

If you find out, let me know.

Someone in my friend group has been acting dubious. Can I trust them?

Yes, but then say "I told you so!" when they betray everyone else

How do I deal with stress?

Go to the gym and then stop because pain does not help

How do you have fun without substances?

Those cookies with the little picture inside them might as well be a Class A substance

What resources are there for me to deal with my deteriorating mental health?

Your university cares so much about your mental health until you actually need something done about it so... Zocdoc has a health insurance filter!

What do I do if my roommates are inconsiderate?

Bring your poor RA into it and find that it will never resolve itself. Clean everything yourself because you will do it better than they would anyway. :(
